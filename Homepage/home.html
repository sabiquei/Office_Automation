Request flow is automated here

In education, whether it's a large university or a local school district when it comes to managing workflows, accuracy and accountability are critical. Educational institutions deal with a tremendous amount of paperwork and regulations that must be managed daily. By automating the workflow around things like hiring, grant proposals, vendor management, capital expenditure and expense requests, centralized approvals, operations requests, etc. paper and manual processes disappear, leaving a more efficient and productive organization in its place. Here,we are introducing an efficient approval automation technique which which can be a very useful application for any college.

request submission


In our college ,usually students used to take the no-due forms and other personal requests as a manual copy.Because of this ,they cannot get the approval of higher authority on time in emergency situations. Sometimes ,they need to spend many days to get their request approved. This time delay of approval can be limited by this technique.Also ,For higher authority , even though they are not available at that day, the request can get approved as everything is digitized.On every semester-break, students have to take the hardcopy of no-due form and wander allover the college for the signature of Head of every departments. It’s tiresome for every students and also is a little-bit annoying for HODs.This can be completely removed with the introduction of no-due form automation. In-case, if there is fine, go there and remit the fine-fee. Then only they get approved from respective departments.Every students who had completed the course, had to come to the college for collecting their caution-deposit. They may need to keep aside all their busy works and has to spend a whole one day for the refund. Then also ,they may not get it on time. They may be asked to come on another day. Approval automation technique makes its best advantage in this case. Students can submit it directly to principal, and they’ll get a reply. After getting this reply, they can go directly to college for collecting the caution deposit. This realistic problem can be better solved by this approval automation technique. Students can submit their request by online and higher authority can approve and send it back to the dashboard of students. Thus, the valuble time of both can be saved. By replacing slow, manual processes with an automated workflow, students can be moved through their processes faster and improving their experience .
